Roadmap & Services

Soil Fertility Testing

Soil fertility tests are paramount for any successful harvest as they evaluate the nutrient supplying power of land. FreshFields aims to make this test painless, efficient, and thorough. *We are currently only taking requests in California.

GAP Consultation

GAP (Good Agricultural Practices) certification is a USDA audit program through which producers can demonstrate their compliance with food safety requirements to purchasers and retailers. This can be a significant blocker for successful farms--we offer a thorough consultation to help prepare for a GAP Audit.

Roadmap
